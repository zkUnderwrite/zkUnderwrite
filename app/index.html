How a private loan happens

Four steps. The income data never leaves the first one.

step 1

Issuer signs

A bank or payroll provider signs your income statement with its key.

private · your device
step 2

You prove

A RISC Zero zkVM verifies the signature and checks income ≥ threshold — producing a proof, not a disclosure.

private · your device
step 3

Contract verifies

The Soroban contract checks the Groth16 proof against the exact program it expects.

public · Stellar
step 4

Credit unlocks

A USDC credit line is disbursed from the lender pool. Nullifier blocks reuse.

public · Stellar
